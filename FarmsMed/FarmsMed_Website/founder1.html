Abhishek Chaubey

Founder

Abhishek Chaubey is a distinguished professional with expertise in leadership, strategic management, and operational excellence. He holds a Postgraduate degree in Management from IIM Raipur and a degree in Political Science from Delhi University.

Abhishek worked with a top FMCG company, where he contributed to strategic initiatives that enhanced production efficiency and strengthened multi-state operations. He also gained valuable experience at the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, where he managed awareness campaigns and collaborated with local stakeholders to promote environmental sustainability and community engagement.

As the Founder of FarmsMed, He brings together strategic vision and social purpose to drive innovation in the agro and veterinary medicine sector. His experience in managing multi-sector projects and building effective community collaborations enables him to bridge business strategy with grassroots impact. Under his leadership, FarmsMed is focused on empowering farmers, improving productivity, providing peace of mind and delivering science-based solutions that ensure healthier livestock, sustainable aquaculture, and a prosperous rural economy.
