Animesh Anand

Co-founder

Animesh Anand is a dynamic professional with expertise in marketing strategy, brand management, and digital transformation. He holds an MBA in Sales and Marketing from IIM Raipur and a B.Tech from NIT Silchar, reflecting a strong blend of analytical and creative acumen.

Animesh has over five years of diverse experience across Marketing, Business Development, and Presales in the IT and Retail sectors, where he successfully led data-driven campaigns, brand development initiatives, and customer engagement programs. His work with leading global enterprises helped deliver measurable improvements in market positioning and business growth.

At FarmsMed, he plays a pivotal role in driving growth through insight-led innovation and strategic communication. His focus lies in building a trusted and impactful brand, aligning marketing initiatives with organizational goals, and expanding FarmsMed’s presence across domestic and international markets. Under his leadership, FarmsMed’s marketing vision is centered on farmer empowerment, technological advancement, and sustainable rural progress.
