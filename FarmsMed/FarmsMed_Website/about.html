About FarmsMed

FarmsMed is a new-age agri-tech and biosolutions enterprise dedicated to transforming the future of aquaculture, dairy, and agriculture through science, sustainability, and social innovation.

Led by a dynamic team of IIM and IIT alumni and a representative from the United Nations, FarmsMed combines world-class expertise with grassroots understanding to create meaningful change at the farm level. Our leadership’s global perspective, deep scientific insight, and unwavering commitment to rural upliftment drive every initiative we undertake.

We develop and deliver advanced farm-care solutions — from scientifically formulated sanitizers, probiotics, and water conditioners to eco-friendly growth enhancers and soil improvers — all aimed at ensuring Cleaner Water, Healthier Harvests, and Prosperous Farmers.

As part of our expansion, we are soon launching a range of herbal and Ayurvedic medicines, designed to offer natural, holistic, and side-effect-free treatments that align with our vision of health — for both farmers and their ecosystems.

At FarmsMed, we believe that true progress begins with the well-being of those who feed the world. Through innovation, compassion, and collaboration, we strive to empower farmers with peace of mind, prosperity, and pride.

Vision

To transform farming into a smart, sustainable, and farmer-first ecosystem — through innovation, care, and trust built on science and tech.

Mission

To deliver holistic farm solutions that prevent disease, enhance growth, and simplify pond and farm management, ensuring that every farmer experiences peace of mind, profit, and progress.
