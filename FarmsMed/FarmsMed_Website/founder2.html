Shakti Singh

Managing Director

Shakti Singh is a visionary leader and strategic thinker with expertise in leadership, governance, and policy execution. A graduate of the Faculty of Law, University of Delhi, he brings analytical depth, legal understanding, and organizational insight to his professional endeavors.

His leadership journey includes serving as President of the Delhi University Students’ Union (DUSU), and member of Kisan Morcha where he spearheaded impactful initiatives in governance, administration, and community engagement. He has also represented India at global platforms such as the United Nations and COP 29 contributing to discussions on sustainable development and farmers welfare — experiences that shaped his commitment to inclusive growth and policy-driven change.

At FarmsMed, Shakti serves as a Managing Director, guiding the company’s outreach, partnerships, and policy alignment. His diverse background enables him to bridge governance principles with on-ground agricultural innovation, ensuring that FarmsMed’s mission — improving farmers’ lives through science-backed, sustainable solutions — is achieved with integrity, impact, and long-term vision.
